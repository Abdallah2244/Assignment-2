ALL ADMISSIONS TO ACCRA TECHNICAL UNIVERSITY ARE STRICTLY ONLINE

SALES CENTRES

E-VOUCHER SERIAL NUMBER AND PIN CAN BE OBTAINED FROM:

a. FOR ALL MOBILE NETWORKS, DIAL THE SHORT CODE *840* 100# AND FOLLOW THE PROMPT TO PAY
AND RECEIVE YOUR E-VOUCHER BY SMS

b. ALL BRANCHES OF ACCESS BANK

c. ALL BRANCHES OF BANK OF AFRICA

d. ALL BRANCES OF FIDELITY BANK

e. INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS SHOULD SEND AN EMAIL TO info@atu.edu.gh APPLICATION VOUCHER
WILL BE GENERATED AND SENT BACK IN RESPONSE TO THE EMAIL.



ONLINE ADMISSION INSTRUCTIONS

1.Purchase your e-voucher from any of the sales centers

2.Log on to the admission system at https://application.atu.edu.gh with e-voucher serial number and pin

3.Follow the steps on the online admissions system to complete your application.

4.Please note: use a passport size (160x160px dimensions) picture with sea blue background.

5.ubmit completed application online and keep a copy of summary page for personal reference.

THE COST OF E-VOUCHER FOR THE VARIOUS PROGRAMMES IS AS FOLLOWS

1.Master’s Degree (MTech/MSc) – GH₵ 200.00

2.Bachelor of Technology (BTech) – GH₵ 200.00

3.Higher National Diploma (HND) – GH₵ 200.00

4.Mature Applicants – GH₵ 200.00

5.Non-Tertiary – GH₵ 200.00

6.Pre-HND – GH₵ 200.00

7.Professional Programmes – GH₵ 200.00

8.International Applicants – Free

MATURE APPLICANTS ARE TO NOTE THAT:

THE COST OF TUITION (CLASSES) AND THE MATURE ENTRANCE EXAMINATION IS GH₵ 600.00

ELIGIBLE MATURE APPLICANTS ARE REQUIRED TO PAY THE SUM OF GH₵600.00 AT ANY BANK OF
AFRICA, FIDELITY, OR ACCESS BANK BRANCHES, COVERING THE COST OF TUITION (CLASSES), AND
MATURE ENTRANCE EXAMINATIONS.

ORIENTATION AND TUITION (CLASSES) FOR MATURE APPLICANTS WOULD BE ANNOUNCED FROM TIME
TO TIME.

Closing date for online application and submission of completed form is September 30, 2025.

NB: All applicants should upload a passport size (160x160px dimensions) picture with sea blue background
when filling the online admission.
